Gulab Jamun

Key Ingredients

  • Khoya
  • Sugar Syrup
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, flour, and a pinch of baking soda.
  2. Form into balls and deep fry until golden brown.
  3. Make sugar syrup with water, sugar, and cardamom.
  4. Soak fried balls in syrup until they absorb it.

Barfi

Key Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Khoya
  • Flavourings

Recipe

  1. Heat condensed milk and sugar until thick.
  2. Add flavorings like cardamom or nuts.
  3. Spread mixture in a tray and let it cool.
  4. Cut into squares or diamonds before it sets completely.

Rasmalai

Key Ingredients

  • Panner
  • Milk
  • Saffron

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, add lemon juice to curdle.
  2. Strain paneer (cheese) and knead into balls
  3. Boil milk with sugar, saffron, and cardamom.
  4. Add paneer balls, simmer until they expand and absorb flavors.

Jalebi

key Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour(maida)
  • Yogurt
  • Sugar Syrup

Recipe

  1. Mix all-purpose flour and yogurt into a batter.
  2. Rest batter, then pour in hot oil in circular motions.
  3. Cook until crispy, then dip in sugar syrup.
  4. Serve hot or cold.

kheer

Key Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Boil rice in milk until soft.
  2. Add sugar, cardamom, and nuts.
  3. Simmer until thickened to pudding consistency.
  4. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with more nuts if desired.
